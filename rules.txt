RULES
------------------
1.You can can choose from 5 categories, or let the computer decide
2.You can choose from Easy (10 lives), Medium (8 Lives) or Hard (6 Lives)
3.You only have to guess the letters 
  (or numbers if they are Roman Numerals (i.e. I or V))
4.If there are numbers (e.g. 1,2,3 etc.) or symbols etc., 
  they will be already visible to the user
5.Spaces are replaced by the "-" symbol
